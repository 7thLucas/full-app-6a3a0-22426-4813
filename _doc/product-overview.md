# Product Overview

## Core Concept

A minimal-touch social media management app for people who hate being on social media but need to maintain a presence — professionals, founders, creators, and businesses who need consistent posting without daily platform engagement.

## The Problem

Reluctant social media users need a consistent online presence for professional or business reasons but find social media platforms draining, distracting, or anxiety-inducing. The typical result: sporadic posting, ghosted accounts, and missed business opportunities. The algorithm punishes inconsistency; human psychology punishes obligation.

## The Solution

A focused tool that collapses a week's worth of social media presence into one short, intentional session:

1. User inputs raw thoughts (text notes, links, topics)
2. AI drafts platform-ready posts from those inputs
3. User reviews and approves drafts in minutes
4. App auto-publishes on schedule — no further action needed

The user never has to open a social media feed.

## Target User

**The Reluctant Poster** — someone obligated to maintain a social media presence (for business, brand, or career) who finds the platforms aversive. They want results without the scroll.

## Core Features

- **Idea Dump**: capture raw thoughts, links, or topics in any form
- **AI Content Drafting**: transforms ideas into platform-optimized post drafts in the user's voice
- **Review & Approve**: fast approval workflow — minimal friction, maximum control
- **Smart Scheduling**: set a publishing cadence once; the app handles the calendar
- **Auto-Publish**: posts go live on time without manual action
- **Multi-Platform Publishing**: LinkedIn, Instagram, X — platform priority TBD from user

## Value Proposition

One weekly session. Consistent daily presence. No feed-opening required.

## Brand Tone

Calm, direct, anti-friction. Speaks to reluctant users who are pragmatic about social media — not enthusiasts. No hype, no "engagement growth" language.
